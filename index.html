Apostle Victor M. Ssenyonga is the founder and vision bearer of Worshippers Love Ministries which started in 2016 after he had just graduated from the University. He began with a group of three young men and two young ladies in the home of one of the members among which four of them were students at University.
He is also commonly known as the Apostle of worship and he is married to Tryphine Ruth Ssenyonga. After one year and two days, the Lord led Victor to launch a fellowship known as worshippers fellowship which he did, by faith carrying the mandate of God upon his life he stepped out.
